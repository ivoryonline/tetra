About this Site

Why'd I make this site? I realised how much I was relying on AI (a lot); and more importantly, — how much less competent it had made me. So I decided to create this site — Tetra!, which uses ZERO Artifical Intelligence in it's design & creation. The general purpose of this site is just to accommodate any content I want to publicise onto a website, whilst having my own place that I can kinda call my digital home! Why'd you name it Tetra? It's just the first word that I thought of when I needed a name for this site; went with it and also searched up what Tetra meant, found a Wikipedia article — decided they were pretty cool and went with a Pristella maxillaris (a type of Tetra) as the favicon!
